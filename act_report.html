7- Visualizing Distribution of Retweet Count VS Favorite Count¶

In [10]:
retweet_log10 = all_details['retweet_count'].apply(lambda x: np.log10(x))
favorite_log10 = all_details['favorite_count'].apply(lambda x: np.log10(x))
plt.hist(retweet_log10, alpha=0.7, bins=200, label='Retweet Count')
plt.hist(favorite_log10, alpha=0.7, bins=200, label='Favorite Count')
plt.title('Distribution of Retweet Count vs. Favorite Count (Log10)', size=15)
plt.legend(loc='upper right', fontsize=12);

From the above chart, we can see that users favor the tweets more than they retweet them.
